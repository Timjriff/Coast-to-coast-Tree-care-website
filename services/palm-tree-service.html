Palm tree service

Palm trimming that keeps properties neat and maintained.

Coast to Coast Tree Care trims palms, removes dead fronds, and helps keep South County landscapes clean and presentable.

Palm trees need regular maintenance to look their best.

Dead fronds, seed pods, and overgrown palm skirts can make a property look messy and create clean-up issues around yards, driveways, pools, and walkways.

Palm service can include:

  • Dead frond removal
  • Palm skirt clean-up
  • Seed pod removal
  • Clean-up around the tree
  • Routine maintenance for homes and managed properties
