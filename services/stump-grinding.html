Stump grinding

Remove the stump and reclaim the yard.

Stump grinding helps clear leftover stumps after tree removal so the area is safer, cleaner, and easier to landscape.

Stumps can be more than an eyesore.

Old stumps can get in the way of mowing, landscaping, new planting, pathways, and general yard use. Grinding the stump helps restore the area without leaving a large obstruction behind.

Stump grinding is useful for:

  • Post-removal clean-up
  • Landscape improvements
  • Reducing trip hazards
  • Opening up usable yard space
  • Preparing an area for new planting or hardscape
