About the company

Local tree care for South Orange County since 2016.

Coast to Coast Tree Care helps homeowners and property managers keep their trees maintained, safe, and clean with practical service and direct communication.

Built around reliable tree work and clean properties.

Tree service is not only about cutting branches. It is about protecting the property, improving clearance, managing hazards, and leaving the yard cleaner than it started. Coast to Coast Tree Care focuses on the kind of tree work local homeowners need most: trimming, removal, pruning, palm maintenance, stump grinding, and urgent clean-up.

The company serves Mission Viejo, Laguna Niguel, Rancho Santa Margarita, Lake Forest, Dana Point, and nearby South County communities.

What customers can expect:

  • Clear communication before the job
  • Practical recommendations based on the property
  • Attention to safety and access
  • Tree work focused on appearance, clearance, and clean-up
  • Local service from an established South County company
